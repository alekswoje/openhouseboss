Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 16, 2026

Foyer ("we", "us") is an iOS app built for real-estate agents to capture open-house conversations, identify visitors, and draft follow-up emails. This policy explains what the app collects, why, and how it's stored.

What we collect

  • Audio recordings of open-house conversations you choose to record. Capture only starts when you tap Record and stops when you tap End Session.
  • Transcripts generated from those recordings.
  • Visitor contact info (name, email, phone) that visitors enter on the kiosk sign-in screen or that you add manually.
  • Your account info — name and email from your Google Sign-In, used to identify your sessions and send emails on your behalf when you grant the Gmail send scope.
  • Property listings you create inside the app (address, price, photos, marketing copy).

What we do with it

  • Transcribe audio via AssemblyAI to identify distinct speakers and what they said.
  • Analyze the transcript via Anthropic Claude to tag each visitor (Buyer / Seller / Browser), score their interest, and draft a personalized follow-up email.
  • Send follow-up emails from your own Google account (only after you explicitly grant the gmail.send scope), with the body you reviewed and approved.
  • Store sessions on our backend so you can re-open them on a different device and so we can re-analyze recordings if the pipeline improves.

What we don't do

  • Sell or share your data, transcripts, or audio with advertisers.
  • Use your data to train AI models. AssemblyAI and Anthropic process your audio/transcripts on a request-by-request basis; neither uses Foyer's data for model training.
  • Track your location, browse other apps, or access your contacts outside what you explicitly type in.

Visitor consent

You are responsible for obtaining recording consent from visitors. The kiosk sign-in screen includes a recording-consent checkbox that visitors must check before joining a session. Local and state laws on consent vary — please follow the rules where you're recording.

Storage and security

Audio recordings, transcripts, and session metadata are stored on our backend (hosted on Render in Oregon, USA) on an encrypted persistent disk. Visitor contact info and your account info are stored in the same backend. Authentication uses JSON Web Tokens signed with a server-side secret; all network traffic is TLS-encrypted.

Deletion

You can delete an entire session — recording, transcript, analysis, and leads — from the Session Detail screen by tapping the Delete button. Deletion is immediate and permanent.

To delete your account and all associated data, email gfxefhe@gmail.com from the address you signed in with and we will purge everything within 7 days.

Third-party services

Foyer uses these services to function. Their privacy policies apply to data they receive from us:

  • Google (Sign-In + Gmail send)
  • AssemblyAI (transcription)
  • Anthropic (analysis + drafting)
  • Render (backend hosting)
